What We Do?

Sustainable, Original, Handcrafted.

COCO is a homegrown brand from TamilNadu that repurposes coconut waste into lasting, sustainable and handmade home products.
Founded in the year 2000 by Karishma,Akkshara,Sriramdharnish,Chandarshini. COCO INDIA PRODUCTS today is an established brand retailing all over India. The natives of TamilNadu, Karishma, Akkshara, Sriramdharnish and Chandarshini were always convinced that coconuts are more than just coconut oil and water. They naturally started looking at the byproducts of coconut processing units and that's how it all began. From making their first coconut shell bowl to exporting abroad, The team and Company have come a long way.
